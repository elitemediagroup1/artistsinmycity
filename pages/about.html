About ArtistsInMyCity

A photo-first discovery platform built to help local artists get seen, help fans find work they love, and help cities showcase their creative voice.

What ArtistsInMyCity Is

ArtistsInMyCity is a premium, city-first platform for discovering local creative talent. Every artist gets their own polished online exhibit built around their work, not a cluttered feed. We are designed to feel like Spotify meets MoMA: a dark, photo-forward experience where the art comes first and every city has a stage of its own.

For Artists

Claim a premium profile that puts your portfolio front and center. Upload your work, tell your story, connect your links, and show up in city and category discovery. Artist profiles, dashboards, and publishing tools are rolling out so you can present your craft the way it deserves to be seen.

For Fans

Follow the artists and work you love, save previews, and explore your city's creative scene by category and neighborhood. Discover live music, gallery openings, and events, and be first to know when new local talent goes live.

For Creators

Photographers, videographers, curators, and promoters help cities find their creative voice. Creators can spotlight artists, cover events, and build the visual identity of a local scene, with dedicated tools and a creator dashboard.

For Cities

Each city becomes a curated creative guide, an artist hub organized around real neighborhoods and art forms. We build city-first, not as a generic directory, so local culture stays at the center of discovery.

Powered by EMG LOOP™

ArtistsInMyCity runs on EMG LOOP™, our event and engagement engine that connects discovery, publishing, and analytics across the platform so artists, fans, and cities stay in sync.

Elite Media Group

ArtistsInMyCity is an Elite Media Group company. Elite Media Group builds premium media and technology products that help creative communities grow and get discovered.
