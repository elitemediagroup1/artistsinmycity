Our Story

Every city is full of artists the world hasn't met yet.

ArtistsInMyCity exists to change that — one exhibit, one stage, one city at a time.

Somewhere near you, a painter is finishing a canvas no one will see. A singer is playing to a half-empty room. A sculptor, a poet, a dancer — pouring everything into work that deserves an audience.

We believe the problem was never talent. It was discovery. So we built a photo-first home where local artists get a premium digital exhibit, fans follow the work they love, and every city gets to celebrate its own creative voice.

This isn't another profile site. It's a stage.

Who it's for

For Artists

Your own premium exhibit — gallery, music, video, events, and store — built to be discovered, followed, and booked.

For Fans

Follow the creators you love, discover new ones nearby, and be first in the room when something incredible opens.

For Creators

Photographers, videographers, and curators who help artists shine — and help cities tell their creative stories.

For Cities

A living creative guide to every city — venues, galleries, districts, and the artists who make it unforgettable.
